Thank you for placing an order with the UW Alumni Store!
 
 
Our order-processing time is five to seven business days (Monday through Friday) for ground shipping and store pickup; or one to two business days for expedited orders. Your order will be shipped via UPS®, FedEx®, or USPS®, which should take one to three days (depending on the location and shipping method chosen) to reach you. Those placing orders for store pickup will receive a notification when the order is ready for pickup at one of our University Book Store partner locations.
 
Payment Processing
 
Credit Card Orders
When you place an order, your bank places an authorization hold on your credit card. This hold secures the funds, and your credit card will not be charged until your order ships. If your order is canceled for any reason, the hold on your card will clear within 72 hours.
 
Debit Card Orders
Although we do not charge your debit card until the order ships, banks place a reserve against your bank account when your order is placed.

The exact amount of time until funds are cleared is determined by your bank or credit card company, so the time may vary.
 
Gift-card Orders
If you are using one (or more) eligible University Book Store gift cards, our system will check with your credit or debit card provider to confirm that there are sufficient funds available to cover the total amount of the order. (Your financial institution might label this fund check with a Pending notation). This happens even if you have entered a gift card(s) with a balance that covers the entire cost of the order. When your order is processed, our system will apply the amount on the gift card(s) first. If that amount covers the total amount of the order, your credit or debit card will not be billed for any amount. If the gift card covers only a portion of the order total, your credit or debit card will be billed the difference. Only if the gift card has no balance would the entire amount of the order be billed to your credit or debit card.
